Project Oracle is a critical underwater robotics module within Project Evo, designed and engineered by Peterson Robotics & Innovation. Its primary function is to transmit energy, haul minerals, and assist deep-sea robotics operations, acting as the central nerve line between surface units and those working on the ocean floor.

10kWh battery, wireless/docked charging, 100kg payload capacity.

Oracle is a compact, spherical, mid-water operations robot designed for high-precision energy distribution, environmental scanning, and underwater logistics. It acts as a mobile liaison between surface units like Indigo and deep-sea modules like Spyder V1 and Land Sharks.

Dielectric oil chambers for battery enclosures ensure batteries and other sensitive pieces are waterproof for longer uses.

Goals

  • 48-hour continuous operations within 500 meters (Planning on making these numbers better.)
  • 5km field logistics
  • Renewable recharge integration over years at a time with cooperation of indigo
  • Mesh-network data relay
