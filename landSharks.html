The Land Shark

Land Shark is a modular, amphibious crawler designed to navigate and manipulate challenging terrains. It can harvest local aggregates and use them to 3D-print rapid-set cement structures on-site. This capability allows for the construction of barriers and other structures in otherwise unsuitable habitats.

Features

  • Swarm capabilities with other land sharks as well as the entire ecosystem enabling for exponential possibilities.
  • Amphibious capabilities for diverse environments as well as full functionality on land.
  • 3D printing technology for on-site construction and material processing
  • Advanced terrain navigation and manipulation

    • Goals

    • Print barriers and structures in otherwise unsuitable habitats
    • Use ≥80% on site materials
    • 0.5 m³/hr print throughput
    • more now than ever, we are utilizing several methods of obtaining energy in ways that help nature instead of fight it.
