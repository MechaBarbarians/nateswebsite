Zephyr is the aerial logistics and surveillance unit in the Peterson Robotics & Innovation ecosystem. It is a drone-based platform designed for long-range package delivery, atmospheric data collection, and aerial oversight of operations taking place on and beneath the ocean.

High-endurance hybrid electric/solar drone for remote supply delivery.

Functionality

  • 5kg payload capacity
  • 20km or more autonomous range
  • 2h endurance, solar-extended and upgradable with dual energy/battery swap
  • GPS and LiDAR obstacle avoidance
  • Secure mesh-network telemetry

Goals

    Work with an already existing satelite network to talk to humans anywhere on the planet.. or some places off it.

  • Reliable remote medical supply drops.
  • Semi-autonomous operation in even extreme environments due to deep learning implementations.
  • Integration with Oracle for seamless logistics and mesh like data interface.
  • Mid-air battery swap demonstrations allowing payloads to be delivered to great distances away.
