Our Story and values

From a Salt Lake City workshop to global field trials.

Peterson Robotics & Innovation exists to pioneer advanced autonomous systems that can explore, build, and sustain in environments where humans cannot. Our mission is to engineer adaptable, eco-synchronized robots—from the ocean floor to the skies above—to solve today’s hardest challenges and prepare for humanity’s future in space and deep-sea frontiers.

Project Evo: Ecosystem for Versatile Operations

Project Evo is a unified robotics ecosystem bridging sky, surface, and seafloor. It integrates aerial delivery (Zephyr), surface energy collection and command (Indigo), mid-water operations (Oracle), terrain scouting (Spyder V1), and deep-sea construction (Land Sharks), all working together to create autonomous, self-sustaining systems for exploration, mining, and innovation for the future. Discover our modular robotics ecosystem and how they work together to create new environments where life can thrive, harvest materials, and research new technology along the way.
