About Us

Our Origin

Founded in 2024 by Naethaniel “Nate” Peterson, our company began with 3D-printing robotics and creating my own software then waterproofing them from scratch.

-

Mission & Values

Project EVO is an acronym for Evolution, yes. One step further though, we are an Ecosystem for Versatile Operations. Bringing the environment with us and building a new one when needed.

Peterson Robotics & Innovation exists to pioneer advanced autonomous systems that can explore, build, and sustain environments where humans cannot. Our mission is to engineer adaptable, eco-synchronized robots—from the ocean floor to the skies above—to solve today’s hardest challenges and prepare for humanity’s future in space and deep-sea frontiers.

We plan to begin Sales of the first mesh network module with active live support in september of 2027.


We are committed to:

  • Open-source development
  • Community engagement
  • Environmental sustainability
  • Real-world testing

We believe in the power of collaboration and innovation to create a better future for all.
