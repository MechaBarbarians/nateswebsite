About Us

Our Origin

Founded in 2024 by Naethaniel “Nate” Peterson, our company began with 3D-printing shoreline barriers using local materials.

Mission & Values

We build sustainable, modular robotics with an open-source ethos. Our core values: Integrity, Sustainability, Inclusivity, Adaptability, Innovation.

Community Engagement

Hosting workshops, “Bot Beach Day,” and collaborating with NGOs and universities for real-world testing.
